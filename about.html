About TechTalk

TechTalk was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide clear, practical, and up-to-date information about web development and programming.

Our team of experienced developers and technical writers work together to create content that helps developers of all levels improve their skills and stay current with industry trends.

We believe in learning by doing, so our articles always include practical examples and real-world applications.
